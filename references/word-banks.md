# Word Banks

Core vocabulary for name construction. Use these as raw material — combine across clusters,
not just within them. The most interesting names often mix distant clusters (e.g., Moss + Engine,
Stone + Cloud, Velvet + Cart).

## Colors (semantic, not decorative)

| Color | Associations | Good for |
|---|---|---|
| Black | Authority, mystery, precision | Security, enterprise, legal |
| White | Clarity, purity, simplicity | Legal, health, clean SaaS |
| Blue | Trust, calm, depth | Finance, health, infrastructure |
| Green | Growth, sustainability, nature | Climate, agriculture, wellness |
| Red | Urgency, warmth, energy | Security alerts, fast-moving ops |
| Gold | Value, premium, legacy | Finance, premium B2B |
| Quiet / Pale | Restraint, elegance | Luxury SaaS, premium tools |
| North | Direction, clarity, cold precision | DevOps, fintech, navigation |

## Compound Starters (high-frequency, high-energy)

These words work especially well as the **first element** of a two-word name:

Glass · Black · Stone · Signal · Iron · Moss · Paper · Blue · Red · North ·
Hidden · Deep · Clear · Wild · Quiet · Second · Night · Green · White · Bright
